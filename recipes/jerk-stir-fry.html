Caribbean Jerk Stir Fry

Eat it.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup sliced sweet onions
  • 3/4 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons Caribbean jerk seasoning
  • 1/2 cup plum sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted peanuts

Cooking Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large skiller over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir bell pepper and onion in the oil until slightly tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Remove pepper and onion from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet, season with jerk seasoning, cook and stir until no longer pink.
  5. Pour the plum sauce in with the chicken.
  6. Add the bell peppers and onions, toss to combine.
  7. Cook until the peppers and onions are heated completely, 3 to 5 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with soy sauce and chopped peanuts to serve.
