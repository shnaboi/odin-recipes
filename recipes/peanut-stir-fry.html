Spicy Thai Peanut Stir Fry Recipe!

An easy-to-cook recipe, served over rice and if you got that TJ's thai peanut sauce, you know what to do.

Ingredients (Sauce)

  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Ingredients (Stir-Fry)

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound skinless, bonelss chicken thighs, cut into small pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1/3 cup dry roasted peanuts
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Cooking Directions

  1. Mix chicken broth, soy sauce, cornstarch, sugar, vinegar, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl until sauce is smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat; saute chicken and garlic until chicken is no longer pink, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Add red bell pepper and saute until slightly tender, about 1 minute.
  4. Stir sauce into chicken mixture and cook until thickened, about 1 minute.
  5. Stir peanuts into chicken mixture and garnish with green onions.
  6. Eat it up! Enjoy!
