Chicken Fajita Pizza Recipe

A delicious, crispy Chicken Fajita Pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16oz) pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 (5 oz) skinless, boneless chicken breasts, sut into small pieces.
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (add more to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped sweet onion
  • 1 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 2 cups chredded Monterey Jack cheese
