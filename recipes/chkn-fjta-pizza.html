Chicken Fajita Pizza Recipe

A delicious, crispy Chicken Fajita Pizza.

This is a specialty pizza that you and your family or friends can enjoy! Can easily be made vegetarian by substituing chicken or cooking without it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16oz) pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 (5 oz) skinless, boneless chicken breasts, sut into small pieces.
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (add more to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped sweet onion
  • 1 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 2 cups chredded Monterey Jack cheese

Cooking Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Roll pizza dough and place on large baking sheet or pizza stone. Prick with fork.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven ultin firm, about 8 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken, Stir for 5 minutes. Stir in garlic, chili powder, and salt until incorporated. Add onion and bell peppers; cook until onions and peppers are tender, ~5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  5. Use a slotted spoon to remove chicken and vegetable mixture from the skillet. Spread over the pre-baked crust. Spoon salsa over top and sprinkle with Monterey Jack cheese.
  6. Return to the oven and bake until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbling. (~15 to 18 minutes.)
  7. Let cool for 2-3 minutes, then enjoy!
